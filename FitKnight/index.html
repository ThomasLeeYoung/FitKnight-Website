About


FitKnight is a mobile app that rewards your better health with experience points and in game gold to make a fantasy character you create stronger to fight friends, monsters and bosses.

Collect Loot

Collect Armor, weapons, and gold by walking, running, biking or being active. Get rare items by reaching daily, distance goals set for you.

Adventure

Virtually explore through dangerous but highly rewarding dungeons by putting yourself in QUEST MODE. While staying active, watch out for random events that you'll have to handle . The longer the activity, the higher a chance you have to find rare items

Battle Monsters, Bosses, & Friends

Fight all kinds of monsters, huge bosses and even your friends. The battle system is made to be engaging and unpredictable. You will never have the same battle twice.

Raid dungeons

Turn on RAID MODE and get active to adventure through seasonal dungeons filled with special monsters, bosses, and items. Dungeons will change constantly so you'll never expect what comes next. You'll want to team up with friends for this.

Battle in local
and worldwide
tournaments

Monthly tournaments will be held to see who is the strongest in your area. Climb the leaderboards and get special items depending on where you place.

Start a clan

Chat with members, challenge other clans Be the strongest in your area and maintain on top for special rewards.

It's simple to get started

Answer basic questions about your health

Weight, height, age, activity level & health goals etc.

Create your character

Customize your look and clothing. save gold or quest for armor and weapons.

Start your activity

Watch your XP bar go up with the amount of steps you are taking. Earn more gold with more steps.

Add friends, get strong & adventure

Add your friends, get stronger. team up and explore dungeons. Buy, sell, & trade items with friends.
